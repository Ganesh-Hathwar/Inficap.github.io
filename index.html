About

about Inficap

InficapTM is a registered LLP. Inficap is founded by two young stock market traders and enthusiasts, for enthusiasts and aspiring traders of all ages.

Only 2% of the Indian adult population invests in the stock market, as against the United States (56%) and China (7%) - Business Standard - Bloomberg article. Much higher participation can be achieved by broad basing financial education among the youth of India who forms 1/5th the total population.

We at Inficap feel that it is our responsibility to help fellow youth become financially independent too. Inficap is driven by the motto of providing financial education to the youth, to help them achieve FINANCIAL INDEPENDENCE.
